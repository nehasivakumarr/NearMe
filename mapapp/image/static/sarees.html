KANCHIPURAM

Nalli Silk Sarees

Kanchipuram Silk House is an establishment associated with the production and sale of authentic Kanchipuram silk sarees, a renowned type of handwoven silk originating from the city of Kanchipuram in Tamil Nadu, India. These sarees are known for their rich, vibrant colors, intricate designs often inspired by temple architecture, and the use of pure mulberry silk and genuine zari (gold or silver threads).
